Como Precision is operated by Suzhou Como Precision Materials Co., Ltd. We handle business, technical, and project information in a lawful and responsible manner.

## Scope

This policy applies when you browse this website, contact us by email or messaging channel, request quotation support, or send drawings, CAD files, material specifications, project notes, or inspection requirements.

## Information We May Collect

Information you provide may include name, company, email, phone, country or region, project requirements, material targets, drawings, CAD files, quantities, lead-time expectations, and technical questions.

Website usage data may include page views, browser type, device information, approximate IP-based region, performance logs, and cookie or analytics data where enabled.

## How We Use Information

We use information to respond to inquiries, route projects to the right technical platform, review manufacturability or supply requirements, prepare quotation follow-up, improve website content, and comply with legal or regulatory requirements.

We do not sell personal or project information.

## Sharing and Security

We share information only when necessary and legally permitted, such as with service providers, logistics partners, inspection partners, or legal authorities when required.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss.
